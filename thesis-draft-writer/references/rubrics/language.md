# Language Rubric

- Prefer precise academic Chinese over ornate phrasing.
- Remove translationese and vague intensifiers.
- Keep terminology stable across chapters.
- Do not let rhetorical novelty outrun actual evidence.
- Avoid mechanical transition chains such as `首先/其次/最后/此外/另外/总之` when they do not carry real logic.
- Avoid empty emphasis shells such as `值得注意的是` or `需要指出的是` unless they introduce genuinely necessary qualification.
- Prefer objective subjects such as `本文` `实验结果表明` `数据显示`.
- Keep正文 paragraph-driven; do not let bullet lists replace analytical prose.
